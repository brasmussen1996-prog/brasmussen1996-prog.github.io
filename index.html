THE THE KRAKEN

7 % ABV • DARK STOUT

Sailors from Skagen have for generations shared tales of the fearsome sea monster, the Kraken, said to lurk in the deep waters off the coast. To honor this mythical creature, HAVBRYG have crafted THE KRAKEN - a robust stout with deep roasted malt, notes of coffee, chocolate, and a hint of sea salt. Each sip takes you on a journey through the Kraken’s ominous waters. Raise your glass and take a deep breath of the enchanting aroma, for in this brew lies both adventure and peril hidden within.

KEY INGREDIENTS

  • Water
  • Roasted Barley Malt
  • Hops
  • Yeast

FLAVOR NOTES

  • Dark Coffee
  • Cocoa
  • Sea Salt

SIRENE SIRENE SAISON

6 % ABV • BELGIAN - STYLE SAISON

The coasts of Skagen and the Danish seas have been the center of myths about mermaids for centuries. These creatures of beauty and mystique are said to seduce sailors with their song. To capture the spirit of these legendary beings, HAVBRYG have created Sirene Saison - a refreshing beer with subtle spicy and fruity notes. Each sip is like an enchanting journey through the legends of time. Let yourself be seduced by Sirene Saison, which honors Skagen’s maritime heritage and the magic of the sea.

KEY INGREDIENTS

  • Water
  • Barley Malt
  • Wheat
  • Hops
