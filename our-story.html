OUR OUR

STORY

Rooted in the maritime heritage of Skagen and shaped by ancient ocean myths, HAVBRYG was born between light and legend.

WHERE LIGHT

MEETS LEGEND MEETS LEGEND

Along the shores of Skagen, the iconic grey lighthouse stands as a silent guardian against the vast sea. For generations, it has guided sailors through dark waters - waters said to hide creatures of myth and mystery.

Inspired by both the steadfast light of the lighthouse and the legends whispered beneath the waves, HAVBRYG was born between reality and imagination.

THE DEPTH OF THE DEPTH OF

THE KRAKEN

In the darkest depths of the sea dwells the Kraken - a symbol of strength, power, and the unknown. Its presence inspires bold character and intensity, reflected in our darker brews.

THE SIREN THE SIREN

Where the sea meets the horizon, legends speak of sirens whose voices carried across the waves. Their elegance and allure inspire lighter, vibrant brews with depth beneath their surface.
